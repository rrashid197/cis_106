### Table 1 
|Directory            |Function                                                       |
|---------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
|BIN                  |ESSENTIAL COMMANDS                                             |
|DEV                  |DEVICE FILE                                                    |
|ETC                  |SYSTEM CONFIGURATION                                           |
|HOME                 |USER HOME DIRECTORIES                                          |
|MEDIA                |MOUNT POINT FOR REMOVABLE MEDIA,SUCH AS DVDs AND FLOPPY DISKS  |

|command|What is does                                    |Syntax        |Example           |
|-------|------------------------------------------------|--------------|------------------|
